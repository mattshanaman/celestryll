# Privacy Policy for BadlyDefined

**Effective Date:** February 20, 2025  
**App Name:** BadlyDefined - Word Puzzle Game  
**Developer:** Ambient Games

---

## Overview

BadlyDefined is an offline word puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect and use.

---

## Data Collection and Usage

### 1. **Local Game Data (Stored on Device Only)**

We collect and store the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Game Progress:**
  - Puzzles completed
  - Current streak count
  - Total points earned
  - Difficulty preferences
  
- **User Statistics:**
  - Number of puzzles solved
  - Average attempts per puzzle
  - Best performance metrics
  - Hint token count

- **Optional Email Address:**
  - Used only for account recovery if provided
  - Never shared with third parties
  - Can be deleted at any time

**Storage Method:**
- All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device
- Encryption key is device-specific and stored securely
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud backup of game data

---

## Data We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ No personal identifying information (beyond optional email)
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No contact information
- ❌ No device identifiers
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No advertising identifiers

---

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.**

Your game data never leaves your device except when:
- You explicitly use the "Share Result" feature (shared text only, no personal data)
- You explicitly use the "Email Stats" feature (only if you provide email)

---

## Data Security

- All local data is encrypted using device-specific encryption keys
- Encryption keys are stored using platform secure storage APIs
- Database is protected from unauthorized access
- No network transmission of sensitive data

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (visible in Profile section)
- **Delete** your data (uninstall the app removes all data)
- **Opt-out** of optional features (email is optional)

---

## Data Retention

- Game data is retained until you uninstall the app
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data
- We do not maintain backups of your data on external servers

---

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The game is designed to be safe for all ages and contains:
- No in-app purchases
- No social features
- No external links (except this privacy policy)
- No advertising (in free version)

---

## Third-Party Services

**Current Third-Party Libraries:**
- SQLite (local database, no network access)
- .NET MAUI (Microsoft, no telemetry enabled)
- CommunityToolkit.Maui (open source, no telemetry)

**No Analytics or Advertising SDKs are currently integrated.**
