Education

Math that shows its work

The order of operations is one of the most fundamental concepts in mathematics — and one of the most commonly misapplied. Pemdas doesn't just give you an answer; it walks you through each step, colour-codes each operation, and explains why the math works out the way it does.

Whether you're a middle-school student learning for the first time, a parent helping with homework, or an adult who just wants to double-check, Pemdas is your transparent arithmetic companion.

Features

Designed for learners of all levels — from first timers to refresher seekers.

👣

Step-by-Step Solver

Watch each operation resolve in sequence, with the active step highlighted at every stage.

🎨

Color-Coded Operations

Each PEMDAS layer gets its own colour so you always know exactly what's being evaluated.

📝

Plain-Language Explanations

Every step includes a brief explanation written in plain English — no jargon required.

📋

History & Review

Clean, distraction-free layout suitable for classroom use and homework sessions.
