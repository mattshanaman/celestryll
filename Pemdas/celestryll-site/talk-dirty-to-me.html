Your brain goes there. That's the game.

Talk Dirty to Me presents a prompt. The prompt sounds one way. You're supposed to say a word. A completely innocent word. The one your brain definitely thought of first. Sure it did.

Cleverly constructed to be PG-13 at every level — the game is cheeky by design, not by accident. It's a party game built for the group that's just mature enough to know exactly what they're implying.

Prompt:

"Something long, firm, and great with butter."

Answer: Baguette. Obviously.
