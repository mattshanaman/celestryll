Sleep & Wellness

Rest is a skill. Let us help you practice it.

Somnaural combines the science of auditory relaxation with a beautifully simple interface. Whether you need white noise to drown out a noisy apartment, gentle rainfall to wind down after a long day, or binaural beats to slip into a meditative focus state — Somnaural has you covered.

Unlike subscription-based sound apps, Somnaural works entirely offline after install. No login. No recurring fees. No algorithm deciding what you hear. Just you and the sounds you choose.
