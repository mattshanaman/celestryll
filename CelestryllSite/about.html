About Celestryll

A small studio with a straightforward mission — build software that actually makes people's lives a little better.

Our Mission

At Celestryll, we believe the best software disappears into the background — doing exactly what you need without demanding attention or advertising its presence. We call this thoughtful engineering: designing from the user's perspective first, and building technically second.

Our apps are small in scope and large in intention. We ship focused tools that solve real problems for real people — whether that's getting a good night's sleep, understanding a math concept, or winning a party game argument.

The Celestryll Name

Celestryll is a coined word — a blend of celestial and a sense of crystalline precision. It speaks to the dual nature of what we build: apps that feel expansive and capable, yet refined and exact in how they work.

The brand lives in Phoenix, Arizona — a city that itself occupies a fascinating space between vast desert sky and precise modern architecture. That tension between the infinite and the detailed inspires everything we create.
