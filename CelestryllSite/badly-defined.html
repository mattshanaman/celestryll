Language is wonderfully imprecise

Every word has shades of meaning. Some have dozens. Badly Defined exploits that glorious ambiguity to create a game that's part trivia, part debate, and part pure absurdity. Read a definition — then figure out what word it's (poorly) describing.

Play solo for a brain workout, or pass the phone around and watch friendships tested when someone insists their answer is technically correct. (It probably isn't. But that's half the fun.)

Definition:

"That thing you do when you sort of know the answer but also not really."

How to Play

1

Read the Definition

A definition appears — carefully crafted to be accurate but wildly imprecise.

2

Pick Your Answer

Choose from multiple options — some obviously wrong, some suspiciously plausible.

3

Debate It

In party mode, argue your case before the timer runs out — half the fun is the discussion.

4

Find Out

Reveal the answer, enjoy the explanation, and move to the next round.
