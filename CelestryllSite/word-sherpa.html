Word & Puzzle

Words don't travel in straight lines

Traditional word searches hide words in straight lines. Word Sherpa throws that out. Words can start anywhere and travel in any direction — diagonal, zigzag, snaking, looping back on themselves. The path is never predetermined.

The result is a more satisfying, more surprising solve. Finding a word that twists through the grid is a genuinely different experience — and the themed puzzle packs keep every session feeling fresh.

Features

A word puzzle experience built for discovery — not just completion.

🔀

Free-Path Word Finding

Words can travel in any direction and take any shape — no forced straight lines.

↗️

All Path Types

Straight, diagonal, zigzag, loop, and snake paths — every puzzle uses them all.

🗂️

Themed Puzzle Packs

Explore packs around nature, travel, food, history, science, and more.

😌

Relaxing Solve Loop

No punishing timers by default — Word Sherpa is designed to be a calm, satisfying experience.

⚡

Speed Challenge Mode

Want a challenge? Enable timed mode and race to find every word before the clock runs out.

📶

Offline Play

Downloaded packs play fully offline — no Wi-Fi required once the pack is on your device.
