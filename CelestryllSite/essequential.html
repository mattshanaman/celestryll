Logic & Patterns

Find the rule. Complete the sequence.

Essequential presents a sequence — numbers, shapes, colors, sounds — and asks one question: what's the rule? Every puzzle is a small act of discovery. Once you see the pattern, the answer becomes obvious. Getting there is the whole point.

Puzzles escalate from clean arithmetic progressions to compound multi-variable sequences where the rule isn't what you think it is. Essequential is built for people who like their brain to work.
